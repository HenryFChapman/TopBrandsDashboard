Infegy Trust Score

Weekly Post Volume

Post volume shows how many social media users talked about this particular brand throughout the year.

Weekly Net Sentiment

Net sentiment shows how brand sentiment changed over time throughout the year. As positive events occured, the brand will show a higher percentage of brand sentiment, while when negative events occured, that sentiment average will drop.

Top 10 Topics

This shows frequently occuring phrases social media users mention when talked about a particular brand. Topics are displayed on a spectrum with darker greens being more positive, and lighter greens being less positive.

Infegy's Narratives

Narratives uses an all-new dynamic algorithm, able to understand and make sense of wildly varied content, from the shortest tweets to the longest articles. Instantly, you can now understand the layers of interconnectedness between a wide variety of documents, not just trivial similarities found between posts. To put it simply, Narratives finds connections between content to reveal a holistic view of the conversations around your topic.
